Eminem Bio

Eminem (real name Marshall Mathers) is an American rapper, song writer, record producer and cited as one of the greatest and most influential rappers of all time. He rose to prominence under the alter ego Slim Shady. He starred in the film 8mile, which is a semi-fictionalized version of his early life. As a record producer, he has brought some big names to the field of music, including 50 Cent, Yelawolf, and Obie Trice. He is among one of the best-selling music artists of all time and was the best selling artist in the United States in the 2000s.
